Health | by W. H. Coefield



This book contains Lectures that were delivered at the Rooms of the Society-of Arts, under the auspices of the Trades' Guild of Learning and of the National Health Society. They have been corrected from the shorthand writer's notes, and are published in almost exactly the form in which they were given. The Sanitary apparatus described can be studied at the Parkes Museum of Hygiene in University College, London.
